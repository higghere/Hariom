About Us

Hariom Industries Limited is a public limited company established in 1998, headquartered in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh — a prominent player in India's Kraft paper manufacturing industry.

Founded by Shri Surendra, Shri Rajendra, and Shri Virender Gupta, we manufacture 100% eco-friendly Kraft paper from recycled waste, serving Northern and Central India with a daily capacity of 400 MT.

Our fully automated facility in Bhauti Pratappur produces Kraft paper across 3 burst factor grades (18 BF, 22 BF, 24 BF), in GSMs from 80 to 180 — built on 26 years of expertise and a commitment to sustainable manufacturing.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What Kraft paper grades do you manufacture?

We manufacture Kraft paper in three burst factor grades — 18 BF, 22 BF, and 24 BF — all available in GSMs ranging from 80 to 180. Custom GSM variations are also available based on your specific requirements.

What is your daily production capacity?

Our manufacturing facility in Bhauti Pratappur, Kanpur Nagar has a daily production capacity of 400 metric tonnes. Our fully automated production lines ensure consistent quality and efficiency across every batch.

Which regions of India do you supply to?

We primarily serve the Northern and Central regions of India, with customers across Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, and neighbouring states. Contact us to discuss delivery to your location.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes. All our Kraft paper products are 100% eco-friendly, manufactured by transforming recycled waste paper into high-quality paper products. This circular approach minimizes environmental impact and supports sustainable industry practices.

How can I place a bulk order or request samples?

You can reach us by phone at +91-9081111119 or email us at hariom@hariomgroup.in. We welcome bulk inquiries and are happy to provide samples of specific grades and GSMs before you commit to a full order.

Our Story
Who We Are

A Legacy of Quality Paper Manufacturing

Hariom Industries Limited (HOIL) is a public limited company founded in 1998 by Shri Surendra Gupta, Shri Rajendra Gupta, and Shri Virender Gupta. Headquartered in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, we are one of the most prominent players in India's Kraft paper manufacturing industry.

From a regional supplier to a nationally recognised manufacturer, HOIL has grown steadily — always grounded in integrity, innovation, and a deep respect for the environment.

What We Make

Kraft Paper for Every Application

We manufacture and market Kraft Papers serving customers across Northern and Central India. Our products are used in industrial packaging, corrugated boards, stationery, and textbook production.

Our facility at Bhauti Pratappur, Kanpur Nagar runs fully automated production lines with a daily capacity of 400 metric tonnes — producing Kraft paper in 18 BF, 22 BF, and 24 BF grades, all available in GSMs from 80 to 180.
