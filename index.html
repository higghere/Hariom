About Us

Hariom Industries Limited (HOIL) is a public limited company established in 1998, headquartered in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. Founded by Shri Surendra Gupta, Shri Rajendra Gupta, and Shri Virender Gupta, HOIL is a prominent player in the Kraft paper manufacturing industry with its state-of-the-art facility in Bhauti Pratappur, Kanpur Nagar.

We are a leading manufacturer and marketer of Kraft Papers, serving customers nationwide across Northern and Central India. Our products find extensive use in industrial applications, packaging materials, stationery, and textbook production. Strategically located, HOIL exemplifies environmental responsibility by transforming recycled waste paper into 100% eco-friendly paper products.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What Kraft paper grades do you manufacture?

We manufacture Kraft paper in three burst factor grades — 18 BF, 22 BF, and 24 BF — all available in GSMs ranging from 80 to 180. Custom GSM variations are also available based on your specific requirements.

What is your daily production capacity?

Our manufacturing facility in Bhauti Pratappur, Kanpur Nagar has a daily production capacity of 400 metric tonnes. Our fully automated production lines ensure consistent quality and efficiency across every batch.

Which regions of India do you supply to?

We primarily serve the Northern and Central regions of India, with customers across Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, and neighbouring states. Contact us to discuss delivery to your location.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes. All our Kraft paper products are 100% eco-friendly, manufactured by transforming recycled waste paper into high-quality paper products. This circular approach minimizes environmental impact and supports sustainable industry practices.

How can I place a bulk order or request samples?

You can reach us by phone at +91-9081111119 or email us at hariom@hariomgroup.in. We welcome bulk inquiries and are happy to provide samples of specific grades and GSMs before you commit to a full order.
